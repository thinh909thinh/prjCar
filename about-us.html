Launched in 1998 and headquartered in Chicago, the Com pany empowers shoppers with the data, resources and digital tools needed to make informed buying d ecisions and seamlessly connect with automotive retailers. In a rapidly changing market, Cars.com en ables dealerships and OEMs with innovative technical solutions and data-driven intelligence to bette r reach and influence ready-to-buy shoppers, increase inventory turn and gain market share. NGUYEN DINH HIEN
HIEN NGUYEN serves as the chief executive officer of Carbazeery.com Inc. (CARBAZEERY). As one of the founding members of Carbazeery.com, HIEN has helped shape the Company from its initial concept into a leading digital marketplace and platform powering innovative solutions and frictionless omni-channel experiences for the automotive industry. Prior to becoming chief executive officer, HIEN served in a number of senior management roles for Carbazeery.com, including president, senior vice president and chief operating officer.
NGUYEN BA QUOC
QUOC has served as President and Chief Commercial Officer for Cars.com Inc. (CARBAZEERY) since January 2022, and served as Chief Revenue Officer before that since July 2018. He oversees the Company’s sales channels and revenue operations, building on customer relationships with nearly 20,000 local automotive dealers as well as major dealer groups, national brands and OEMs. QUOC is leading the transformation of the go-to-market organization to help expand the Company from a listings model to a business where media, digital solutions and data all work together to support customers’ goals and growth of the business.
MAI XUAN TIEN
TIEN was appointed Chief Innovation Officer of CARBAZEERY.com Inc. (CARBAZEERY) in January 2022. As Chief Innovation Officer, Joe is responsible for driving organic and inorganic innovation across CARBAZEERY’ enterprise platform. With a passion for computer programming and moving metal, Joe co-founded Dealer Inspire in 2013 and served as CEO, leading the development of new solutions to help dealers win. In a few years, Dealer Inspire has grown to close to 800 employees serving over 5,400 clients and 42 OEM brands with game-changing innovation as part of the CARS family of companies.
TONG MINH DUONG
DUONG has served as Chief Product Officer at Cars.com Inc. (CARBAZEERY) since January 2022. He oversees the Company’s product, design and research organizations. In this role, DUONG is focused on delivering CARS’ platform strategy by providing the best experience for car buying and selling. In his career, DUONG has built teams at early-stage venture-backed startups and has scaled teams at Fortune 100 enterprises to drive digital transformation at scale. Prior to joining CARBAZEERY, he led commercial technology and innovation for Sysco Corporation and had general management responsibilities for Sysco’s digital innovation arm, Sysco LABS. Previously, he held various product roles at Front Gate Tickets (acquired by Ticketmaster), Civitas Learning, and SpareFoot (now Storable).
